Crystal Collector

You get a random number at the start of the game.

Four crystals below. Click a crystal and it will add points to your total score.

Win by matching your total score to random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

Crystal value is hidden from you until you click it.

When new game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal. Have Fun!!!
